Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Effective Date: February 5, 2026

Welcome to BatchMate ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Important: By using BatchMate, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you register and use BatchMate, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, unique user ID, password (encrypted), profile photo URL
  • Profile Information:
    • Students: Mobile number, class/grade, school/college name, location (city, state), bio, date of birth (optional), parent/guardian contact (optional)
    • Teachers: Mobile number, subject specialization, teaching experience, highest degree, location (city, state), bio, certifications, other experience
  • Batch Information: Batch details, subject names, monthly fees, class schedules, descriptions
  • Payment Information: Payment records, due amounts, payment status, remarks (Note: We do not collect or store credit card or banking details)
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through in-app chat, notices, announcements
  • Attendance Records: Attendance status, dates, percentages

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our App, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
  • Usage Data: App features accessed, time spent on screens, interaction patterns, crash reports
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (we do not collect precise GPS location)
  • Advertising Data: Ad impressions, clicks, rewarded ad views for batch limit unlocking

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Sign-In: Email address, name, profile picture (with your consent)
  • Firebase Services: Analytics data, crash reports, performance metrics
  • Google AdMob: Advertising identifiers, ad interaction data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 To Provide and Maintain Our Service

  • Create and manage user accounts (student and teacher profiles)
  • Facilitate batch creation, enrollment, and management
  • Track payments, send payment reminders, and generate receipts
  • Enable communication between students and teachers
  • Manage attendance records and calculate percentages
  • Display notices, routines, and group links
  • Provide analytics and reports to teachers

2.2 To Improve Our Service

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Monitor app performance and fix bugs
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Conduct research and analytics

2.3 To Communicate with You

  • Send payment reminders and notifications
  • Notify about batch invitations and join requests
  • Send important updates about the App
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

2.4 For Advertising

  • Display personalized advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Track ad impressions and manage rewarded ad credits
  • Comply with advertising partner requirements

2.5 For Security and Legal Compliance

  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations and government requests

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 Within the App

  • Teachers can see: Student names, user IDs, profile photos, mobile numbers, attendance records, payment status of students enrolled in their batches
  • Students can see: Teacher names, profile information, batch details, notices, routines
  • Batch Members: All members of a batch can see each other's names and profile photos in group chats

3.2 With Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf:

  • Firebase (Google): Cloud hosting, database, authentication, analytics, crash reporting
  • Google AdMob: Advertisement delivery and performance tracking
  • Cloud Functions: Automated tasks, notifications, data processing

3.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to:

  • Valid legal requests from government authorities
  • Court orders or subpoenas
  • Protection of our rights, property, or safety
  • Investigation of fraud or security issues

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored on Firebase Cloud Firestore servers, which may be located in various regions globally. Firebase complies with international data protection standards.

4.2 How We Protect Your Data

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption of data at rest (Firebase encryption)
  • Firebase Authentication security rules
  • Secure password hashing (never stored in plain text)
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and role-based permissions

4.3 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, your data is soft-deleted and retained for 30 days (grace period for account recovery). After 30 days, data is permanently deleted.
  • Legal Retention: Some data may be retained longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes
